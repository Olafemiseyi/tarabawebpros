Is this beginner friendly?

Absolutely! This cohort is designed specifically for people with no prior experience in coding or website design. We'll guide you step-by-step from scratch.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. This is a 100% no-code program. We will use a powerful visual builder tool that lets you create professional websites without writing a single line of code.

Will I get a certificate of completion?

Yes, upon completing the program and a final project, you will receive a professional certificate of completion from LightOrb Innovations.

Do I need a laptop?

Yes, a laptop or a desktop computer is required for this training. While you can watch the video lessons on a smartphone, the hands-on building exercises can only be completed on a computer.

What about hosting and domain fees?

No. We will teach you the Minimum Viable Product (MVP) approach, which includes free options to launch and host your website without any initial costs.
